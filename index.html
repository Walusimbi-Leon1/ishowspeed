About This Book

IShowSpeed: The Biography is the most comprehensive account ever written about Darren Jason Watkins Jr. — the kid from Cincinnati who became the biggest livestreamer on the planet. From his early days streaming to ten viewers on a PlayStation 4 camera, to performing at the 2026 FIFA World Cup closing ceremony, this book traces every chapter of Speed's extraordinary journey.

Covering his childhood, his explosive rise, his music career with Warner Records, his world tours, his WWE debut, his viral moments, his controversies, and his enduring impact on internet culture, this is the definitive story of the man behind the energy.
